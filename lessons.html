- LESSONS -

My lessons are tailored to each student's skill level. I focus on fundamentals first, then progress to complex techniques. Lessons include video feedback, play-along exercises, and personal goal tracking to maximize improvement.

Take a look at the lessons I offer to decide which will be the best for your needs!

Beginner Drumming Basics
