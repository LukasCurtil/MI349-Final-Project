Practice Metronome

Consistent practice with a metronome is essential for developing solid timing and rhythm. This tool will help you:

  • Build steady tempo control
  • Improve your internal sense of timing
  • Develop muscle memory for complex patterns
  • Track your progress as you increase speed

Pro Tip: Start slow and focus on accuracy before increasing tempo. It's better to play correctly at 60 BPM than sloppily at 120 BPM!
