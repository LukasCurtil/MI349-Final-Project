FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long are the lessons?

Standard lessons are 60 minutes, though 30-minute sessions are available for younger students or those focusing on specific skills. We can discuss what works best for your schedule during our first conversation.

Do I need my own drum to take lessons?

While having your own practice pad or snare drum is helpful for home practice, it's not required to get started. I can recommend affordable options if you're ready to purchase equipment.

What are your rates?

Lesson rates vary depending on session length and frequency. Contact me for current pricing and package options. I offer discounts for students who book multiple sessions in advance.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes! I offer both in-person and online lessons via video call. Online lessons include the same quality instruction with video feedback and personalized practice materials.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

Bring your sticks, a practice pad if you have one, and any music you're currently working on. Most importantly, bring your questions and enthusiasm to learn!

Can you help prepare for specific auditions?

Absolutely! I specialize in audition preparation and can help you prepare for high school, college, or independent group tryouts. Just let me know what you're auditioning for.
